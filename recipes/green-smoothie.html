The Best Green Smoothie Ever!

Much better than Tropical Smoothie...

Cool, refreshing, and oh-so-healthy! This green smoothie is perfect in the morning for breakfast or a light snack. Packed with leafy greens, healthy fats, and delicious fruits, it's a sweet way to get all of your vitamins and nutrients in!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cup milk (dairy or dairy-free)
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 apple
  • ¼ avocado
  • plus optional add-ins

Directions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to a blender and blend on high for 30 seconds, or until creamy.
